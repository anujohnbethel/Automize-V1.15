HOW IT WORKS

Automize has spent years curating and vetting quality virtual assistants so you can have assurance that the assistants that work on your business will provide consistent quality.

Trust the Process

Schedule
A Discovery Call

Simply put, this is where we understand your business and what makes it tick. Based off this initial call, we are able to get a better understanding on the level of service you will need.

Onbording

Time to welcome you to our team! This is where you will be paired with your Account Manager. Have project management software? Great, we can use this. If not, we will set your team up on Basecamp to begin the project integration.

All important account level aspects will be sorted and refined at this stage and project scope will be defined.

Match Day

It's time to meet your VA’s! Based on your project and business needs, we will provide you with highly curated and skilled VA’s to set your business up for success!

Process Upload

Gone are the days of using Word files to communicate Standard Operating Procedures. We will provide your team with screen recording software where you will upload your current processes that you wish to delegate to your VA’s. Your VA team will study these to ensure they understand and can refine your procedures.

Time to Go Live!

Once your procedures have been studied and refined, it’s time to optimize! Say goodbye to working for your business and hello to working on it!
